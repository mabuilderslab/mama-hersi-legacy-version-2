Sarah Hersi Ali Mama Heri Legacy Website
A museum-grade, dignified tribute website honoring the life and legacy of Sarah Hersi Ali (Mama Hersi/Ayeyo Yurub), pioneer businesswoman of Bungoma, Kenya.

Overview
This is a complete, self-contained HTML website that preserves the story of Sarah Hersi Ali - matriarch, entrepreneur, and community pillar. The site serves as a living archive of her contributions to Bungoma and East African commerce, featuring:

Respectful, archival tone without modern marketing language

Fully responsive design (mobile-first)
